# Feature Research: AI Finance Tracker

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Manual Expense Logging**: Quick-add UI for daily spending.
- **Budget Categorization**: Automatic/Manual tagging (Food, Rent, Fun).
- **Goal Tracking**: Progress bars for "Save for Trip" goals.

## Differentiating Features (The "AI Edge")
- **AI Budget Planner**: Analyzes income vs. goals and suggests "Safe to Spend" daily amounts.
- **Scenario Simulator**: "If I buy this \$50 ticket, my trip savings date moves back 2 days."
- **Portfolio Insights**: AI identifies high-fee funds or suggests diversification based on user risk profile.

## Avoid for v1
- **Real-time Bank feeds**: Too expensive for the current budget.
- **Social finance/sharing**: Complicates privacy and security early on.
